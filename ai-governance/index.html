The AI Operating System Bootcamp is a 5-day immersive program designed for C-suite executives and senior leaders who need to move beyond AI experimentation and build the institutional operating system that integrates AI into governance, strategy, operations, and decision-making. This is not about prompts or tools — it is about enterprise reinvention.

Led by Alejandro Reynoso — Ph.D. from MIT, lecturer at Cambridge Judge Business School, and co-founder of DEFI Capital — this bootcamp delivers the frameworks, governance models, and implementation playbooks that the world's most advanced organizations are deploying right now. Participants leave with a complete AI Operating System blueprint ready for board-level presentation.

The AI Landscape: From Tools to Operating Systems

Understanding the current state of enterprise AI: LLMs, multimodal models, agents, and autonomous systems. Why most AI initiatives fail and what separates experimentation from transformation. Mapping the AI maturity curve for your organization. The shift from AI-as-tool to AI-as-operating-system.

4 Hours

AI Governance & Regulatory Architecture

Building enterprise AI governance frameworks aligned with EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, and emerging LatAm regulations. 03
4 Hours

Enterprise AI Architecture & Infrastructure

Designing the technical architecture for enterprise AI: data pipelines, model orchestration, MLOps, and AI infrastructure. Build vs. buy decisions. Vendor evaluation frameworks. Security, privacy, and data sovereignty considerations. Creating your AI technology stack blueprint.

4 Hours

AI-Driven Strategy & Organizational Transformation
